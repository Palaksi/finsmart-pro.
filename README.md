# 📊 FinSmart Pro: The Ultimate Financial Toolkit

**FinSmart Pro** is a professional, browser-based financial application designed for commerce students (B.Com, CA, CMA) and small business owners. It simplifies complex financial tasks like GST invoicing, income tax computation, and ratio analysis.

🚀 **Live Project:** [https://finsmart-pro.vercel.app/](https://finsmart-pro.vercel.app/)

---

## ✨ Key Modules & Features

### 📈 Ratio Analyzer (CMA/Finance)
- Compute **Current, Quick, and Debt-Equity ratios** instantly.
- Get automated liquidity verdicts based on industry standards.

### 🧾 GST Invoice (Taxation)
- Generate multi-item invoices with automatic **CGST/SGST/IGST** calculations.
- Clean, printable layouts ready for business use.

### ₹ Income Tax Calculator
- Compare **Old vs New Tax Regimes** for FY 2025-26.
- Includes HRA exemptions, 80C/80D deductions, and Rebate 87A logic.

### 🎓 CMA Exam Assistant
- Specialized tools for **Marginal Costing** (P/V Ratio, BEP).
- **Variance Analysis** (Material, Labour, Overheads) with step-by-step working notes.

### 📖 Exam Mode
- A unique feature to pin **Working Notes**—perfect for students during timed practice sessions.

---

## 🛠️ Tech Stack
- **Frontend:** Modern HTML5 & CSS3 (Flexbox/Grid)
- **Logic:** Vanilla JavaScript (ES6) for high-speed local computations.
- **Charts:** [Chart.js](https://www.chartjs.org/) for visual data representation.
- **Deployment:** [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) for lightning-fast hosting.

---

## 🔒 Privacy & Performance
- **100% Client-Side:** No data ever leaves your device. Everything is computed locally in your browser.
- **Built for India:** Follows the latest **ICAI/CMA curriculum** and Indian Finance Act standards.
